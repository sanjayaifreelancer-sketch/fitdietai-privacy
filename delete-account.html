How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your Fit Diet AI account and all associated data in one of two ways:

Method 1: In the App

Open Fit Diet AI, go to Settings, scroll down and tap Delete Account. Confirm the deletion and all your data will be permanently removed.

Method 2: Via Email

Send an email to the address below using the email account associated with your Fit Diet AI account. Include "Delete Account" in the subject line.

indianfitdietai@gmail.com

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, we permanently remove:

  • Your profile (name, email, weight, height, preferences)
  • All food logs and meal history
  • All workout logs
  • Water intake logs
  • Daily step data
  • Weight entries and progress photos
  • Group memberships and messages
  • Badges and achievements
  • Payment and referral records

Data is removed within 30 days of your deletion request.
