Privacy Policy

Fit Diet AI · Last updated June 2026

Information We Collect

When you use Fit Diet AI, we collect the following:

  • Account Information — Name and email address when you register.
  • Fitness Data — Weight, height, age, diet preferences, food logs, workout logs, water intake, and step count.
  • Device Information — Device type and operating system version for troubleshooting.
  • Payment Data — Google Play transaction IDs only. We do not store credit card details.

How We Use Your Data

  • Provide personalized nutrition and fitness tracking
  • Calculate daily calorie and macro targets
  • Power AI features including food scanning and nutrition coaching
  • Process Google Play subscription payments
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs

Data Storage & Retention

Your data is securely stored on Supabase servers. We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If your account remains inactive for 24 months, your data may be automatically deleted.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time using one of these methods:

  • In the App — Go to Settings → Delete Account
  • By Email — Send a deletion request to indianfitdietai@gmail.com using your registered email address

All personal data, food logs, workout logs, photos, and profile information will be permanently removed from our servers within 30 days of your deletion request.

Third-Party Services

We use the following services to operate the app:

  • Supabase — Database, authentication, and file storage
  • NVIDIA — AI models for food image scanning and nutrition coaching
  • Google Play Billing — Subscription payment processing

Children’s Privacy

Fit Diet AI is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
